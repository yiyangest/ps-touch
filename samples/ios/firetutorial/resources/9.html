Give color to fire
Now we bring color to the fire by using Hue & Saturation.
With the "new FIRE" layer selected
Image -> Adjustment -> Hue/Saturation
The goal here is to give the fire an orange/red look to it.

First, click "Colorize" check box.
Next, slide the Hue to the orange area (try 30).
Then bring the Saturation slider to the red area (try 90).
